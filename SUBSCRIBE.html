Subscription Options


The Three Year Plan

Most popular

With this plan you will be donating 15 dollars a month by the end of the three years you will have given a total of 540 USD. and after the 3 years you'll be sent an email to either change you plan to a different one and or end your subscription with us



The One Year Plan

This plan is only for a year and you will donate a dollar a day. By completing this plan you will have donated 365 dollars.This plan can be canceled at any time. After the year you will be sent an email to enroll for the next year or given the option to roll into "Member 14" which gives us access to automatically enroll you in the following year unless contacted to not do so.



The Ten Year Plan

This membership is for ten years and you will be donating85 USD a month. By the end of this membership you will have donated 10,200 dollars. This subscription will earn you a gold pass. The gold pass is for supporters who have given a lot of dedication and support to us. This gold pass will earn you emails of all the projects that have been done and many updates on the improvements we have made. Also this gold pass will earn you an invitation to online meetings where we hear from the public for opinions and ideas of what the next move should be.
